The Best Movies According to Yogesh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Crazy Stupid Love

The underconfident mid-40s person gets divorced and is forced to get back into the dating pool by a young stunning guy.

It gets even funnier when the stud falls in love with his old protege.

Rating - 7.5/10

12th Fail

The struggle of an UPSC aspirant shown in this movie really gets to you to the deepest level and it makes you think how priviledged you really are.

Rating - 9/10

Harry Potter Series

This 8-part movie is a really good for those who admire fantasy world.

A boy unaware of his powers on his way to get revenge and save the world from the most dangerous dark wizard of all time.

Rating - 8.5/10
